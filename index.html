AdhocRedirect

?adhoc&url={manifest_url}

Redirects to iTunes Services URL for adhoc app installation. Converts to: itms-services://?action=download-manifest&url={url}

TrollStore

?trollstore&url={ipa_url}

Redirects to Apple Magnifier URL for TrollStore installation. Converts to: apple-magnifier://install?url={url}
